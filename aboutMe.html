I'm Erika Nordman

She/Her

As a UC Davis alumn, I am passionate about human centered design and making products for people; not just things people use. I am a natural problem solver with an endless curiousity who keeps herself busy and loves to bring fresh energy to the projects I work on and the people I work with. Researching my target audience and really getting an understanding of my client is one of my favorite parts of my design process. Not just a creative job, but also a technical job, I take pride in making personalized products that are a perfect balance of aesthetic and function.

When I am not designing, you can find me backpacking around California, trying new recipes, or getting caught up on my reading list.
